Gallery Closed for Renovations

The editorial board announces that the ArtViea Gallery will remain shuttered for a brief interval as the proprietors oversee substantial improvements to the premises. The refinements, anticipated by late autumn, shall bring forth a refreshed salon worthy of the city’s most discerning collectors.

In the meantime, patrons may peruse our seasonal bulletin for glimpses of forthcoming exhibitions, artist interviews, and feuilletons from the world of art and culture. Though the doors may be temporarily closed, our devotion to fine craft and cultivated taste remains unwavering.

Public Notice: Works shall recommence on the twenty-fifth day of September, with the grand re-opening to be heralded in due course.
